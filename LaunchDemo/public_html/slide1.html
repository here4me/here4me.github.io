What is here4.me?

The here4.me platform allows content producers to deliver digital content and services to the right people, at the right place, at the right time.

What does this mean? Content producers can target their digital content and services to specific content consumers based on who they are, where they are, and when it is.

Q: Who are content producers?

A: Any person or organization that can serve HTML documents via URLs on the public internet.

Q: Who are content consumers?

A: Any person who needs content and services specific to their current context.

Q: What does digital content and services mean?

A: Anything that can be rendered by a web browser such as:

  • Images
  • Videos
  • Audio
  • Simple web pages
  • Complex responsive web applications
Q: What does "...based on who they are..." mean?

A: Anything that can identify a specific individual or group of individuals based on attributes such as:

  • Gender
  • Age
  • Personal preferences
  • Political affiliation
  • Anything a content producer needs to provide the best user experience
Q: What does "...where they are..." mean?

A: Using location services and/or QR codes content producers can identify the exact location of a content consumer such as:

  • State
  • City
  • Neighborhood
  • Building
  • House
  • Table at a restaurant
  • Conference room in a building
  • Anything a content producer needs to provide the best user experience
Q: What does "...when it is..." mean?

A: This can be anything time or event based such as:

  • Exact date and time
  • Specific days of the week
  • Specific hours of the day
  • Current inning of a baseball game
  • Result of a political election
  • Value of specific stocks
  • Status of an airline flight
  • Anything a content producer needs to provide the best user experience
